Sosuke Shima (志摩 聡介) is one of the main characters in Skip and Loafer series.

Appearance.

Sosuke has blond hair. He keeps a smile on his face most of the time even while being pestered to join the drama club by Kanechika.

Personality.

Sosuke is Mitsumi's classmate. He is urban, smart, and good-looking. He is a nice person who is kind to everyone, but he does not show his true feelings and has a somewhat elusive side. He becomes close to Mitsumi after an incident at the entrance ceremony, and is influenced by her hard work in everything, contrary to himself.

Relationships.

Mother: When he was a child their relationship was wholesome, but due to "adult problems" Shima started to try to get attention for his mother by acting, wich later on caused on him a lose of personal indentiy and a pleasing personality, putting other people expectations above him.
